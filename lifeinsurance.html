Questions
Frequently Asked
Is LifeSecure a licensed insurance company?+

LifeSecure is a licensed financial advisory firm regulated by Bank Negara Malaysia. We partner with leading takaful operators and life insurers to bring you the best plans.

How does the WhatsApp consultation work?+

After submitting the recommendation form, a pre-filled WhatsApp message is generated with your profile details. Your consultant will respond within 2 hours during business hours (Mon–Sat, 9am–6pm).

Are the prices shown exact?+

Prices shown are indicative estimates. Your actual contribution depends on your health declaration, occupation, and the specific plan features chosen. Your consultant will provide an exact quote.

What is Hibah Takaful?+

Hibah Takaful is a Shariah-compliant wealth distribution mechanism where you nominate a beneficiary to receive your takaful benefits as a gift (hibah), bypassing the need for a lengthy probate process.

Can I switch plans later?+

Yes. Most plans allow upgrades, add-on riders, or adjustments at your annual review. Your consultant can advise on the best time to make changes based on your life stage.
